Thinking, Fast and Slow

by Daniel Kahneman
9/10

A comprehensive exploration of the two systems that drive the way we think—the fast, intuitive, and emotional system; and the slower, more deliberative, and logical system.

Key Takeaways:
  • Our brains use two primary systems for decision making
  • Cognitive biases significantly influence our daily choices
  • Understanding these systems helps improve decision-making
